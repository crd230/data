--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Codebook for Assignment 4, Question 2 data file philadelphia_tracts.shp
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
Demographic and socioeconomic data were downloaded from the 2015-2019 American
Community Survey
2019 crime data were downloaded from the Philadelphia Open Data portal.
Units of observation: Census tracts
Philadelphia city, PA
Note: Tracts with 0 population were removed
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data Dictionary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
GEOID: Census tract ID

popd: Total population per squared miles

concd: Concentrated disadvantage

immc: Immigrant concentration

resm: Percent of individuals who moved residences in the past year (Standardized)

pocc: Percent of housing units that are owner-occupied

vcrpop: Log violent crime rate (crime per 1,000 residents)
